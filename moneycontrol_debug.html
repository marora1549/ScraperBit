Stock Ideas

This is investment advice on stocks which have been tracked by brokers and researchers. They assign a buy, sell or hold rating on stocks along with a target price ... Read More
from which an upside or downside from the current market price is calculated. Also one can view the current returns which have been achieved till date since the recommendation date. Upon viewing performance, the stock’s performance since recommendation date is compared to the relevant benchmark by standardising the entry price. Alpha is the difference between the stock’s performance and its relevant benchmark performance since the recommendation date. These recommendations can be sorted by the latest ones and by maximum upside potential through the tabs. Also the recommendations can be filtered by upgrades or downgrades in recommendation or price. One can also search a particular company or choose recommendations only from a particular researcher.
